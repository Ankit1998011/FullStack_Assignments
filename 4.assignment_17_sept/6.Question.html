Difference between HTML and HTML 5:

HTML HTML 5
1.Limited support for multimedia (e.g., no built-in video/audio elements). 1.Enhanced multimedia support (e.g., built-in video/audio elements).
2.Traditional and complex syntax. 2.Simplified and cleaner syntax.
3.Relies on generic and elements for structure 3.Introduces semantic tags (eg. like nav, header, footer etc).
